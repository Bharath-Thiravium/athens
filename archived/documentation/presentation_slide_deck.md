## üé¨ **ANIMATION & TRANSITION GUIDE**

### **Slide Transitions**
- **Standard:** Fade (0.5 seconds)
- **Impact slides:** Push from right (0.7 seconds)
- **Data slides:** Morph (0.8 seconds)

### **Element Animations**
- **Text:** Fade in from left (0.3 seconds, staggered)
- **Icons:** Scale up (0.4 seconds)
- **Charts:** Wipe from bottom (1.0 seconds)
- **Statistics:** Count up animation (1.5 seconds)

### **Interactive Elements**
- **Hover effects:** Subtle scale (1.05x)
- **Click animations:** Pulse effect
- **Progress indicators:** Smooth fill animations
